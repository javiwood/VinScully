Vin Scully
Legendary Broadcaster


A brief timeline of Vin Scully accomplishments:

  • 1927 - Born in the Bronx, New York
  • 1950 - Started broadcasting for the Brooklyn Dodgers.
  • 1953 - At 25, became the youngest person to broadcast a a World Series game (a record that stands to this day).
  • 1957 - Dodgers officially move to Los Angeles and Vin Scully joins the club in Southern California.
  • 1976 - His personality became popular that the fans voted him the "most memorable peronality" in the history of the franchise.
